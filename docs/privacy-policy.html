Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

TheOriginals ("we", "our", "the app") is a language-learning app that lets you read literary works in their original language alongside a translation. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Data we collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. The app does not require an account or login.

The following data is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted to our servers:

  • Your language preferences (languages you speak and want to read)
  • Your reading level preference
  • Your reading progress (which chapter and position you last read)

2. Push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging)

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) by Google to send you notifications when new books are added in the languages you selected. To do so, your device is subscribed to anonymous topic channels based on your language preferences (e.g. "Russian → French"). No personal identifier is stored or transmitted to us in this process.

Firebase is operated by Google. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

3. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party, except as described above (Firebase/Google for push notifications).

4. Children's privacy

TheOriginals is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

6. Contact
